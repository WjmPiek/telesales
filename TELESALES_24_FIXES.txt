TeleSales 24 - New campaign creation fix

Fixed a regression introduced by database-backed campaign image storage.
The create route previously required campaign.image_url before the campaign
had been inserted and assigned an ID. Because image_url is generated only
after the first database commit, every new WhatsApp image campaign returned
to the form with the generic missing-image/template warning.

The route now validates campaign.image_data during creation, commits the
campaign, then generates the permanent public image URL from the campaign ID.
